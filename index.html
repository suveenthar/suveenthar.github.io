About Me

From the moment I picked up my first controller, I knew gaming was more than just a hobby—it was a passion. That passion drove me to not only play games but to understand how they work, which sparked my journey into the world of development.

Today, I channel that energy into creating my own interactive experiences and building cool things for the web. As a content creator, I love sharing my process, from the first line of code to the final product, hoping to inspire others to start their own creative journeys.

My Journey

  • 2020 Started my gaming channel on YouTube, building a community around shared passion.
  • 2021 Wrote my first "Hello, World!" and began learning the fundamentals of HTML & CSS.
  • 2022 Dived into JavaScript and created my first playable WebGL game from scratch.
  • 2023 Launched this portfolio to consolidate my projects and share my work with the world!
